## Summary

Updated HGVS variant description recommendations (version 15.11) from the Sequence Variant Description Working Group, operating under HGVS, Human Variome Project, and HUGO. Revisions since 2000 have focused on removing inconsistencies and tightening definitions to allow automatic data processing. Full recommendations at http://www.HGVS.org/varnomen. The standard covers DNA, RNA, and protein variant description syntax.

## Relevance to cdot

HGVS nomenclature is the entire reason cdot exists — it is the clinical and research standard that makes transcript-level variant coordinates necessary. cdot's purpose is to make it computationally tractable to convert between HGVS and genomic coordinates by providing fast, comprehensive transcript data. This paper establishes the standard that cdot serves. Cited in cdot docs/notes.txt.
