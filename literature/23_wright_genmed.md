## Summary

Editorial arguing for widespread clinical adoption of MANE transcript sets to standardise variant reporting. Notes that inconsistent transcript selection creates ambiguity in clinical reports and complicates variant sharing. MANE Select and MANE Plus Clinical provide a solution; the authors call for laboratory standards and reporting guidelines to mandate MANE transcript usage.

## Relevance to cdot

Provides clinical motivation for why canonical transcript lookup (cdot issue #36) matters. Clinical labs using cdot for HGVS conversion need to know which transcript to use — MANE standardises this. cdot's tag storage enables this lookup; the paper argues that tools failing to surface canonical transcripts are creating real clinical problems.
