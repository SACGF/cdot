## Summary

Annual update to the Ensembl genomic resource platform, covering vertebrates and model organisms. Key 2023 updates: expanded reference genome annotations across species, pangenome haplotype annotations, AlphaFold protein structure predictions, updated Variant Effect Predictor (VEP), and a new website. Ensembl is one of the two primary sources (with RefSeq) of transcript annotation data consumed by cdot.

## Relevance to cdot

Ensembl GTF files (releases 81–115) are the primary source for Ensembl transcript data in cdot. The paper documents the annotation releases cdot depends on. Key data: Ensembl's gene annotation pipeline, transcript biotype classification, and GENCODE convergence all affect what cdot ingests. The VEP update is relevant as VEP-compatible cdot releases are a planned feature (issue #63).
