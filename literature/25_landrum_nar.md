## Summary

Major ClinVar update supporting three classification categories: germline variants, oncogenicity classifications for somatic variants, and clinical impact assessments. Now holds >3 million variants submitted by >2,800 organisations. XML and VCF formats updated to accommodate multiple classification types with backward compatibility maintained through 2024.

## Relevance to cdot

Shows the scale and diversity of HGVS-described variants that tools like cdot must handle. The addition of somatic variant classifications introduces new transcript contexts (cancer-specific isoforms, fusion transcripts) that may require extended cdot coverage. The >3 million variant count at >2,800 contributing organisations quantifies the clinical scale of HGVS usage that cdot's infrastructure serves.
